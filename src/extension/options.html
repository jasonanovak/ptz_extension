What is PTZ Extension

PTZ Extension is a Chrome Extension that lets you control the pan, tilt, zoom, brightness, sharpness, contrast, and saturation of your webcam.

How do I get started?

To use PTZ Extension to control your camera you

MUST click the button below

PTZ Extension uses the camera permissions to: 1) display self-view and 2) send the settings you select to the camera.
